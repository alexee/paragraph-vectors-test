An appeal having been taken to this Court by the above-named appellant from a
judgment of the Supreme Court, New York County (Charles Solomon, J.), rendered on
or about November 2, 2009, said appeal having been argued by counsel for the respective
parties, due deliberation having been had thereon, and finding the sentence not excessive,
it is unanimously ordered that the judgment so appealed from be and the same is hereby
affirmed. Counsel for appellant is referred to Rules of the Appellate Division, First
Department (22 NYCRR) § 606.5. Concur—Sweeny, J.P., Renwick,
Moskowitz, Richter and Gische, JJ.
