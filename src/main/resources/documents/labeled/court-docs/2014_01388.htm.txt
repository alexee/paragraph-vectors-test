	Tower Insurance Company of New York,
Appellant,
v
Solomon Knopf et al., Respondents.


—[*1]


An appeal having been taken to this Court by the above-named appellant from an
order of the Supreme Court, New York County (Louis B. York, J.), entered on or about
October 12, 2012, and said appeal having been argued by counsel for the respective
parties; and due deliberation having been had thereon, and upon the stipulation of the
parties hereto dated February 7, 2014, it is unanimously ordered that said appeal be and
the same is hereby withdrawn in accordance with the terms of the aforesaid stipulation.
Concur—Tom, J.P., Friedman, Saxe, Richter and Clark, JJ.
