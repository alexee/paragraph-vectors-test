Application by the appellant for a writ of error coram nobis to vacate, on the ground
of ineffective assistance of appellate counsel, a decision and order of this Court dated
February 14, 2012 (People v
Parker, 92 AD3d 807 [2012]), affirming a judgment of the Supreme Court,
Kings County, rendered July 20, 2009.

Ordered that the application is denied.

The appellant has failed to establish that he was denied the effective assistance of
appellate counsel (see Jones v Barnes, 463 US 745 [1983]; People v Stultz, 2 NY3d
277 [2004]). Skelos, J.P., Leventhal, Lott and Miller, JJ., concur.
