An appeal having been taken to this Court by the above-named appellant from an
order of the Family Court, New York County (Susan K. Knipps, J.), entered on or about
November 18, 2013, and said appeal having been withdrawn before argument by counsel
for the respective parties; and upon the stipulation of the parties hereto dated January 28,
2014, it is unanimously ordered that said appeal be and the same is hereby withdrawn in
accordance with the terms of the aforesaid stipulation. Concur—Gonzalez, P.J.,
Tom, Saxe, Freedman and Manzanet-Daniels, JJ.
